RGB Guessing Game by Integrated Science Club

Did you know that most color that exists can be constructed by only mixing 3 colors? They are Red, Green and Blue.

We Integrated Science Club created this RGB Game for everyone to play around with the three colors, and try to recreate the color we provided.
