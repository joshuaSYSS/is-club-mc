Information


Tennis is a sport played by 2 players through hitting a ball with a racket. The idea behind tennis is hitting the ball, which is an important aspect of physics, a branch of integrated science. So we Integrated Science Club decided to collaborate with Tennis Club to create this game for students to learn more about both tennis and the mechanism of it.
